Magic Cookie Bars aka 7-layer bars

Description

My favorite cookie to make for the holidays

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 1/2 cup graham cracker crumbs
  • 1 cup of chopped nuts
  • 1 cup (6oz) semi sweet chocolate pieces
  • 1 cup (6oz) butterscotch pieces
  • 1 1/3 cup flaked coconut
  • 1 can (15oz) sweetened condensed milk

Instructions

  1. In 13x9 glass pan,melt butter while preheating oven to 325 degrees
  2. Mix graham cracker crumbs with butter & press on bottom of pan
  3. Sprinkle with nuts, chocolate pieces, butterscotch & coconut
  4. Pour sweetened condensed milk over coconut
  5. Bake 30-35 minutes until lightly browned
  6. Cool & cut into bars (Makes about 36 bars)
