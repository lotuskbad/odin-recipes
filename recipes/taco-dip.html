Auntie Donna's World Famous Taco Dip

Description

This is the dip that goes the fastest at a party, double it if you are going there are more than six people. Served with Tostito Scoops

Ingredients

  • 16 oz. Sour Cream
  • 1 can of refried beans
  • 1 package of taco seasoning
  • Scallions
  • Tomatoes
  • Black Olives
  • 1 package of shredded Mexican cheese
  • Iceberg Lettuce
  • Jalepenos (optional)

Instructions

  1. Mix the sour cream and taco seasoning together
  2. Layer the refried beans on the bottom of the glass casserole dish
  3. Layer the sour cream next
  4. Then layer lettuce, scallions, tomoatoes, black olives and cheese
  5. Add jalepenos if wanted
  6. Serve chilled with Tostitos Scoops
